Key Market Drivers for 2026:

Digital-First Personalization: Consumers expect AI-driven recommendations. If a retail product does not have a digital ecosystem, it is currently "under-trending."

Subscription Economy: High-growth retail trends in 2026 are shifting from "one-time purchases" to "recurring consumption models" (e.g., replenishment subscriptions).

Sustainable Premiumization: Consumers are willing to pay a 15–20% premium for products with verified supply chain transparency (ESG compliance).

Research Directive: When investigating trends, prioritize "Social Commerce" impact. If a product has strong traction on short-form video platforms (TikTok/Reels), classify it as a "High-Velocity Trend."
